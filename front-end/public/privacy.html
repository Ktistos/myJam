myJam Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

myJam is a development-stage application for organizing jam sessions, participants, instruments, setlists, and song proposals.

Information We Use

When you sign in, myJam uses the basic account information provided by your sign-in provider, such as your display name, profile photo, and account identifier. The app also stores profile details you choose to enter, such as instruments, bio, available hardware, jam participation, and song or playlist submissions.

How We Use Information

The information is used to operate the app: showing your profile, managing jam membership, assigning roles, displaying proposed and approved songs, and keeping session state synchronized between participants.

Sharing

Information you add to a jam may be visible to other users who can view or join that jam. We do not sell personal information.

Development Notice

This app is currently under active development. Test data may be reset during development, and features may change.

Contact

For privacy questions or data removal requests, contact the project owner through the account that provided access to this deployment.
